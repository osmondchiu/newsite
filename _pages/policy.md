## Overview

My policy work focuses on strengthening public sector capability, improving workforce conditions, and advancing equitable representation in Australian institutions. 

This includes submissions to parliamentary inquiries, engagement with stakeholders, and contributions to public policy debates through research and advocacy.

---

## Parliamentary and Inquiry Work

### Procurement practices of government agencies in New South Wales and its impact on the social development of the people of New South Wales Inquiry (2024)
Submission advocating reforms to improve procurement in New South Wales. Recommended legislative changes to lift transparency and accountability to best practice, adopt social procurement policies and a community wealth building framework.

### Foreign Interference Through Social Media Inquiry (2020)
Submission highlighted how misinformation spreads on WeChat and how it is used for disinformation by domestic political actors.

## Public Sector and Workforce Policy

### Public Sector Capability and Outsourcing
Ongoing work analysing the scale, structure, and capability of the Australian Public Service, including the long-term impacts of outsourcing and contracting models.

### Workforce Conditions and Reform
Policy analysis and advocacy relating to:
- pay and bargaining frameworks  
- future of work in the public sector  
- workforce sustainability
