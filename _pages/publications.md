## Overview

My publications focus on public sector reform, workforce policy, social democracy, and multicultural representation. They span academic research, policy analysis, and commentary across major Australian and international outlets.

---

## Featured Publications

**AI in the Australian Public Service (Journal of Industrial Relations, 2025)**  
Survey-based analysis of how federal public servants view the impact of AI on their work, with implications for workforce capability and future job design.

**Australia’s Public Sector Is Far Smaller Than Debates Suggest (Per Capita, 2025)**  
Challenges prevailing narratives about the size of the public sector and highlights risks to institutional capability.

**The Unexpected Albolanche: Australian Labor’s Historic Victory (Renewal, 2026)**  
Analysis of electoral dynamics and the implications for political strategy and social democratic policy.

**After Fowler: A Turning Point for Asian Australians in Politics? (Peril, 2025)**  
Examines structural barriers and changing dynamics of multicultural political representation.
